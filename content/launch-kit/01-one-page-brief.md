# TerraNexus AI — One-Page Product Brief

## What We Are
TerraNexus AI is an AI-powered real estate intelligence platform that connects verified Indian developers with AI-qualified domestic and NRI investors through source-backed data and transaction-ready Deal Rooms.

## The Problem
Real estate portals show listings, not intelligence. Investors cannot verify data. Developers cannot reach qualified capital. Cross-border investors lack trust.

## The Solution
**Verified Data** — Every project is cross-checked against government registries (RERA, DLD, Land Registry). Source-attributed fields. No fabricated contacts.

**AI-Qualified Buyers** — Every investor is assessed for budget, timeline, and intent before introduction.

**Deal Room Infrastructure** — Structured onboarding, Deal Passport tracking, commission pipeline.

**Institutional Research** — Integrated JLL, CBRE, Knight Frank, Savills, Colliers data.

## Coverage
- 27 Indian cities
- 25+ verified developers
- 11 government & research data sources
- 6 NRI markets targeted (UAE, Singapore, UK, USA, Canada, Australia)

## Revenue Model
3% success fee on closed deals. No listing fees. No subscriptions. Performance-based only.

## Target Users
- Indian developers seeking qualified buyers
- NRI investors seeking verified Indian real estate
- Family offices and institutional investors
- Domestic HNI buyers

## Key Differentiators
1. Source-verified data (no fabricated fields)
2. AI-qualified investor matching
3. Zaha Hadid–inspired brand experience
4. Deal Passport legal infrastructure
5. RERA-integrated verification layer
